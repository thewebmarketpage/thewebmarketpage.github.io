Privacy Policy for Giant Web Market

Last Updated: February 27, 2025

1. Introduction

At Giant Web Market, we respect your privacy. This privacy policy explains our data collection practices, which are minimal by design. We want you to understand that Giant Web Market itself does not collect or store any personal data from our visitors.

2. Our No Data Collection Policy

Giant Web Market does not collect, store, or process any personal data from our website visitors. We do not use contact forms, user accounts, cookies for tracking purposes, or any other mechanisms to gather information about you or your browsing habits.

3. Third-Party Services: Google AdSense

The only data collection that occurs on our website is through Google AdSense, which we use to display advertisements. Google AdSense may use cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites.

Google AdSense may collect information about:

  • IP addresses
  • Cookies and data about which page you are visiting on our site
  • Device identifiers
  • Browser type and settings
  • Information about your activity on our website

This information is collected directly by Google, not by Giant Web Market. For more information about how Google uses this information, please visit Google's Advertising Privacy Policy.

4. Google Analytics

Our website uses Google Analytics to help us understand how visitors interact with our website. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect anonymous information such as the number of visitors to the site, and the most popular pages. We use this information solely to improve our website. The information collected is not linked to any personally identifiable information.

5. Cookies

Our website does not use cookies for tracking or data collection purposes. The only cookies used on our site are those placed by Google AdSense and Google Analytics as described above. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. Most web browsers allow some control of most cookies through the browser settings.

6. Links to Other Websites

Our website may contain links to other websites of interest. However, once you have used these links to leave our site, you should note that we do not have any control over that other website. Therefore, we cannot be responsible for the protection and privacy of any information which you provide whilst visiting such sites and such sites are not governed by this privacy statement.

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update our privacy policy from time to time. Any changes we make to our privacy policy in the future will be posted on this page. Please check back occasionally to ensure you are happy with any changes.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at: alexmarkfor@gmail.com
